¡Hi! I'm Katherine.

I welcome you to my virtual corner...... Here I share a selection of photos that I have captured during my exciting trips to various cities around the world.

If I had to summarize my life in a few words, I would say that every day I value the wonderful experience of existing. Sharing every moment with my family, my husband and my adorable dog "Alma" fills me with joy. Every day I strive to be a better version of myself, seeking happiness and spreading it to those around me.
My passion for photography arose when I began to explore different countries and came across contrasting landscapes. with the beauty of my beloved Bogotá. This experience motivated me to purchase a camera before my next trip, determined to capture the memories that my eyes treasured.
If any of the images in this blog captivate you, do not hesitate to contact me to share your impressions. I hope that, like me, you find something that inspires you to try something new, that awakens your curiosity. Just like I did with photography!
